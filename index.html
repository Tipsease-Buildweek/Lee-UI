Find Your Server

Tipsease contains a list of places and service workers—including restaurant servers, bellhops, valet attendants, and more from around the world—all laid out in an easy-to-read grid format.

Meet Your Server

Tipsease offers profile pages with information about your server. You'll find a thumbnail photo, how long they've been working there, and a tagline to help you remember who they are. You can even send a tip on the page too!

Immediate

No middle men here. When you tip, the funds are transferred from your account to your server's account, 24/7/365.

Supportive

Servers take care of us. Take care of them in return. Tipsease helps ensure that your money goes to who you intend, directly to the hard-working people who deserve it.

Secure

Our encrypted payment gateway provides a secure and trustworthy bridge between you and your server.
